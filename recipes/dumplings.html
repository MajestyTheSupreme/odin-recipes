Dumplings

A dumpling is a small mass of dough that can be boiled, fried, baked, or steamed. There are a ton
of dumpling varieties out there, from pork- and cabbage-filled Chinese dumplings to cheesy Polish dumplings.

Dumpling Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Baking Powder
  • White Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter or Margarine
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Gather the ingredients. Baking ingredients arranged on a white surface, including flour, milk, butter, sugar, salt, and baking powder.

  2. Step 2

    Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Overhead shot of flour and dry ingredients being whisked together for dumplings

  3. Step 3

    Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. Stir in milk and mix until a batter forms that is thick enough to be scooped with a spoon. Allow batter to rest for 3 to 5 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes. Serve.

  5. Step 5

    Serve hot and enjoy!
